Privacy Policy

How CST Institute collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: July 2026 · This is a general template — please review and adapt it to your actual practices and applicable law before publishing.

1. Information we collect

When you fill an enquiry or admission form, we collect your name, mobile number, email, education details and course interest. During admission we also collect documents such as your Aadhaar copy, marksheet and photographs, used solely for enrolment and certification purposes.

2. How we use your information

We use your details to respond to enquiries, process admissions, conduct examinations and certification (including MKCL processes for MS-CIT), inform you about batches, results and relevant offers, and improve our services.

3. WhatsApp and communications

Our website forms open WhatsApp with your details pre-filled; the message is sent only when you press send in WhatsApp. By contacting us, you consent to receive replies and course-related updates via phone, WhatsApp, SMS or email. You may opt out of promotional messages anytime by informing us.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only where necessary — e.g., with MKCL for MS-CIT registration and examination, or with prospective employers when you opt in to placement assistance.

5. Data security & retention

Records are stored securely and accessible only to authorised staff. We retain admission records as required for certification and legal purposes.

6. Cookies & third-party services

Our website may use basic analytics and embedded services (such as Google Maps and YouTube), which operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Your rights & contact

To review, correct or delete your personal information, contact us at info@cstinstitute.in or +91 XXXXX XXXXX.
