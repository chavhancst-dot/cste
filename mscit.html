MS-CIT Course (MKCL)

Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology — the state's most trusted, government-recognised IT literacy course.

// why ms-cit

One certificate, endless opportunities

MS-CIT is conducted by MKCL (Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Ltd.) and is recognised by the Government of Maharashtra. It is required or preferred for most state government jobs and gives you complete confidence with computers, internet, and modern digital skills.

  • Government of Maharashtra recognised certificate
  • Required / preferred for state govt. job applications
  • Covers modern digital skills — not just old MS Office
  • Learn at our authorised centre with personal guidance
