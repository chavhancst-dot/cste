Frequently Asked Questions

Everything students and parents ask us — about admissions, MS-CIT, courses, fees and placements.

// admissions
How do I take admission at CST Institute?
Simply visit the institute, call us, or fill the enquiry form on the Admissions page. After free counselling and a demo lecture, submit your documents and first instalment to confirm your seat.
What documents are required for admission?
Aadhaar card photocopy, latest marksheet, 2 passport-size photos, and your mobile number/email.
Is there an age limit for courses?
No. We train school students, college students, job seekers, working professionals, homemakers and senior citizens.
Can I get a free demo lecture before joining?
Yes! Every student can attend one free demo lecture before paying any fees.
When do new batches start?
New batches start every month for most courses. Contact us for the next batch date of your course.
Can I pay fees in instalments?
Yes, easy monthly instalments are available for all courses. MS-CIT fees follow the official MKCL structure.
Are there any discounts or scholarships?
We offer combo-pack discounts, merit concessions for toppers, and sibling discounts. Ask our counsellor for current offers.
// ms-cit
What is MS-CIT?
Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology — the state's most popular IT literacy course, conducted by MKCL and recognised by the Government of Maharashtra.
Is MS-CIT compulsory for government jobs?
Many Maharashtra government posts require or prefer a recognised computer certificate, and MS-CIT is the most widely accepted one. Always check the specific job advertisement for exact requirements.
What is the duration of MS-CIT?
Approximately 3 months with daily learning and lab practice.
Who can join MS-CIT?
Anyone — there is no strict educational barrier. School students (typically 10th onwards), graduates, employees and homemakers all join.
How is the MS-CIT exam conducted?
Through an official MKCL online examination at the end of the course.
Is CST an authorised MS-CIT centre?
Yes, CST Institute is an authorised MKCL centre for MS-CIT in Bhusawal.
What are the MS-CIT fees?
Fees are as per the official MKCL fee structure, which changes periodically. Contact us for the current fee and instalment plan.
// courses & learning
Which course is best after 10th standard?
MS-CIT + Typing is the classic foundation. Students interested in IT careers can also start Python early. Our counsellor will guide you based on your goal — read our blog guide too.
Which course is best after 12th?
Commerce students: Tally Prime + Advanced Excel. Science/IT-inclined students: Python, Java or Web Development. Everyone benefits from MS-CIT if not already done.
Which programming language should I learn first?
We recommend C for strong fundamentals, or Python if you want the fastest path to job-relevant skills. Both are beginner friendly at CST.
Do I need my own laptop?
No. Our lab provides one computer per student. Having a laptop at home helps practice but is not required.
In which language are courses taught?
Simple Marathi + English mix, so every student understands comfortably.
Do courses include practical training?
Yes — 100%. Every course includes daily hands-on lab time, assignments and projects.
Will I get a certificate after the course?
Yes. MS-CIT students receive the government-recognised MKCL certificate; other courses receive CST Institute certification after assessment.
Do you teach AI and ChatGPT?
Yes! Our AI Tools & ChatGPT course teaches practical AI skills for office work, content creation and productivity.
What batch timings are available?
Morning, afternoon and evening batches, Monday to Saturday. Working students can choose evening batches.
What if I miss some lectures?
We help you cover missed topics in practice sessions or backup batches — just inform your faculty.

What jobs can I get after Tally + Excel?
Accountant, accounts assistant, billing operator, back-office executive and data/MIS roles in local firms, CA offices and businesses.
What jobs can I get after programming courses?
Trainee software developer, web developer, technical support and freelance projects — many students continue to IT roles in Pune/Nashik.
Do you provide internships?
Top-performing students get internship assistance with local businesses and IT firms.
Can I start freelancing after design courses?
Yes — Photoshop and CorelDRAW students often start freelance design work or join local printing/design studios.
