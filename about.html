About CST Institute

Building Bhusawal's digital future — one student at a time, since day one.

// our story

25+ years of computer education in Bhusawal

CST Institute was founded with a simple belief: every student in Bhusawal deserves the same quality of computer education available in big cities. What began as a small classroom has grown into one of the region's most trusted institutes, training 5000+ students in IT literacy, programming and job-oriented skills.

Today CST is an authorised MS-CIT (MKCL) centre and a full-fledged training hub for languages like C, C++, Java and Python, plus professional tools like Tally Prime, Advanced Excel and modern AI tools.
